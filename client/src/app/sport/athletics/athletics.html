• General Rules:
    • Don’t enter in the long jump pit without permission from Athletics Secretary
    • High jump and pole vault Mat should be used with prior permission from Athletics Secretary
    • Leave starting 6 lanes of tracks for Inter-IIT practice session from 7 p.m to 8:30 p.m
    • Vacate discuss throw,javelin throw and shot put throw area during practice time as it may lead to injuries
    • Don’t bring animals in the Gymkhana ground
  • Issue of Equipment:
    • All the Athletics equipments can be issued from SAC storeroom against valid ID card
    • Issued equipments should be used properly and returned after use without any physical damage
  • Rules regarding fine:
    • Fine will be imposed on entering long jump pit without permission
    • Parents of kids playing on mats will be charged ₹ 500
    • Any damage to the issued equipment will incur a fine of thrice the value of the product
